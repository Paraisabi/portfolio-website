    • "The 'Bodies' Studio is a collection of three teams all aimed with different mediums of art that seeks to be provacative, innovative, and hosts minor undertones of grunge and alternative aesthetics.

    The first of the teams is the [Art] team. Bodies of [Art] focuses on public works, installations, partnerships with museums, and galleries. The [Art] team is characterized by their physical, traditional art such as sculptures, paintings, sketches, and the like. The team's works dates back to 2018."

    The second of the teams is the [Music] team. Bodies of [Music] focuses on a variety of audio and varbal art ranging from poems, original songs, covers, instrumentals, and jam sessions. The [Music] team is characterized by their various rock genres, mixing both acoustic and synthesized sounds.

    The third of the teams is the [Design] team. Bodies of [Design] focuses on being commisioned by outsied organizations and agencies. The [Design] team ischaracterized by their posters, websites, logos, and other digital artworks.
