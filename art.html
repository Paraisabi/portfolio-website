    "Sunflowers At Dawn" was a project in collaboration with Bodies of [Music] where the [Art] drew up an album cover for the first EP. The cover features a field of Sunflowers with an expansive background behind a center-focussed flower all draped over the light of "blue hour." While the album is not finalized, this was the first collaborative effort between two creative teams of the "Bodies Studio."
